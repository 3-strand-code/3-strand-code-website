FAQ

What will I learn?

You will learn every step to execute production projects, start to finish. This course will advance you from your current position to the next level. We’ll cover topics such as:

  • Environment and terminal
  • Git and GitHub
  • Editors and IDEs
  • Package managers
  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • JS Libraries & Frameworks
  • CSS Preprocessors
  • Front end Frameworks
  • Working with APIs
  • Scalable Application Architecture
  • Agile Software Development
  • Project Management Tools
  • Build tools & Deployment
  • Automated Testing
  • Continuous Integration & Deployment
  • Hosting and maintenance
  • Analytics
  • Backends as a Service

Will the classes be hands on or lecture?

Classes will be a mixture of both. The first part of the class will consist of an introduction to the topic and the remaining time will be spent practicing and building with the knowledge gained.

What will I work on?

We're doing things differently. You get to build anything you want. On day 1 we'll hold project pitches and collectively vote on projects to build.

This course is open to the ideas of the students and instructors.

Can I work on my own project?

Absolutely, as long as it fits into the scope of the class.

Will I work solo or in a group?

You can work solo, but it’s highly recommended to participate as a group.

What do I need to bring?
  • A laptop or computer
  • Project ideas
  • Insatiable curiosity
Will there be homework?

We believe you learn best by collaborating with other great developers. We're seriously focused on helping you create impressive portfolio material.

So, no. There will be no traditional homework. Instead, you'll be expected to successfully build things. We'll provide all the resources, guidance, and support to ensure you can do this.

When employers look for web developers, they look mostly at your work. You'll most likely be hired to build stuff. We're here to ensure you can confidently do exactly that.

What skill level do I have to be at?

Somewhere between intern and advanced. Our unique process works best with a full spectrum of skill sets and experience levels. Just like a start up or web development shop, you'll play the role that best suits you.
